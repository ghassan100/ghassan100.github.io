Summary

This project utilizes data previously obtained using Python, API, and JSON traversals to present weather changes as function of latitude and away from the equator

During data preparations we used 1,500 random Latitude and Longitudes coordinates, with use of Python CitiPy library we found the city nearest to a given Lat/Lon, we obtained a listing of over 500 cities. Using openweathermap we obtained weather data for those cities; with pandas libraray we constructed a dataframe of weather information for over 500 cities.

The dataframe was saved to CSV and the data is analyzed To show the relationship between latitude and temperature/humidity/cloudiness/wind, utilizing MatplotLib scatter plots to view changes across the three climate zones Tropical Zone, Temperate Zone, and Polar Zone.

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
